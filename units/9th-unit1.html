📚 Reading Comprehension

My New School

My name is Dana. I am 14 years old and I just started 9th grade at a new school. I was very nervous on my first day, but everyone was friendly. My favorite subject is English because I love learning new words and reading stories.

After school, I like to play basketball with my friends. I also enjoy drawing and listening to music. I think this is going to be a great year!
